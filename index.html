How to Deal with Money and Bargain

As in any other country, exchanging money is a sensitive topic, with abusive fares withdrawing money can be come a headache, but we are here for you.

If you have or can create a multicurency account, such as Wise or Revolut, we totally recommend. These accounts can be the savior of your trip when paying less taxes, since they allow you to exchange the money on the account and withdrawal from an ATM on the local currency, not paying the ATM additional taxes.

Places to Drink & Clubs in Istanbul

  • 1. Beyoğlu District - Best known for its bars and clubs
  • 2. Kadıköy - Trendy bars with a local vibe
  • 3. Ortaköy - Stunning waterfront bars and nightclubs
  • 4. Galata - Bohemian cafes and rooftop bars
